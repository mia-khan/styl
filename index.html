The process

From pin to
perfect find

01
Connect Pinterest

Securely link your account in one click. Pick which boards to scan — outfits, wishlist, inspo, anything.

02
Visual matching

A local AI model reads each pin visually — silhouette, color, garment type — and searches across hundreds of retailers.

03
Quality scoring

Every result is scored by material composition vs price. No more guessing if the Shein version is actually the same fabric.

04
Shop smarter

Get a ranked list of dupes with buy links, material breakdowns, and a plain-English verdict on each one.

🧵
Material transparency

See the exact fabric composition of every result — 95% cotton vs 100% polyester — so you buy what you're actually paying for.

💸
True price comparison

Styl finds the cheapest version of each item across ASOS, Zara, H&M, Shein and more — same look, best price.

🔒
Fully private

Everything runs locally on your device. Your Pinterest data, pins, and searches never leave your laptop.

✦
Zero ongoing cost

No subscriptions. No API bills. The AI model runs on your machine, and all searches use free public web data.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

At Styl, accessible at mia-khan.github.io/styl, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Styl and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at privacy@styl.com.

Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

Information We Collect

Styl is a locally-run personal tool. When you use Styl, the following applies:

  • Your Pinterest credentials and board data are stored exclusively on your own device.
  • No personal data is transmitted to Styl servers, because Styl has no servers.
  • If you contact us directly, we may receive your name and email address.

How We Use Your Information

Any information voluntarily provided (e.g. via email contact) is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell, share, or use your data for marketing purposes.

Log Files

GitHub Pages (which hosts this website) follows standard procedure of using log files. These may include IP addresses, browser type, ISP, date and time stamps, and referring pages. This information is not linked to personally identifiable information. See GitHub's data collection policy for details.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies. The Styl application itself runs entirely locally and sets no cookies.

CCPA Privacy Rights

California consumers have the right to request disclosure of personal data collected, request deletion of personal data, and opt out of the sale of personal data. Styl does not sell personal data. To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@styl.com. We will respond within one month.

GDPR Data Protection Rights

You are entitled to the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or port your personal data, and to object to its processing. Since Styl stores no personal data on external servers, these rights are satisfied by design. For any inquiries, contact privacy@styl.com.

Children's Information

Styl does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our contact channels, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the updated date at the top of this page.
